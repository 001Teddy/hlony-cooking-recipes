🍫 Mug Cake

Ingredients

4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoons cocoa powder
1/4 teaspoons baking powder
3 tablespoons milk
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
A pinch of salt
Optional: Chocolate chips or nuts

Procedures

In a microwave-safe mug, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.

Add the milk, vegetable oil and vanilla extract, and stir until smooth.

Optional: Stir in some chocolate chips or nuts.

Microwave the mug on high for about 1 minute to 1 minute 15 seconds.

Let the cake cool slightly, and enjoy straight from the mug!

🍚 Rice Krispies

Ingredients

3 Teaspoons butter
4 Cups mini marshmallows
6 Cups Rice Krispies cereal

Procedures

Melt the butter in a large saucepan over low heat.

Add the marshmallows and stir until completely melted.

Remove from heat and gently stir in the rice krispies cereal until evenly coated.

Press the mixture into a greased 9x13-inch pan.

Let cool, cut into squares, and serve.

🍓 Chocolate-Dipped Strawberries

Ingredients

1 Cup chocolate chips (dark, milk, or white)
12 Fresh strawberries

Procedures

Melt the chocolate chips in the microwave in 20-second intervals, stirring between each, until smooth.

Dip each strawberry into the melted chocolate, letting the excess drip off.

Place the dipped strawberries on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

Allow the chocolate to set at room temperature or refrigerate for quicker results.
