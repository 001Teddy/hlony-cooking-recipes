Potato Salad

Ingredients

6 medium potatoes (Yukon gold or russet)
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon mustard
2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/4 cup chopped red onion
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh parsley and paprika for garnish

Procedures

Boil the potatoes: Peel and cut the potatoes into bite-sized chunks. Boil them in salted water for about 10-15 minutes until tender. Drain and let them cool.

Mix ingredients: In a large bowl, combine mayonnaise, mustard, apple cider vinegar, chopped celery, red onion and eggs. Stir well.

Combine with potatoes: Gently fold the potatoes into the dressing mixture, making sure they are well-coated.

Season: Add salt and pepper to taste.

Garnish: Sprinkle paprika and fresh parsley on top.

Chill before serving and Enjoy!

Classic Potato Salad

Ingredients

6 medium potatoes (Yukon gold or red potatoes)
1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
2 celery stalks, diced
Hard-boiled eggs, chopped
1/4 cup dill pickles, diced (optional)
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Procedures

Cook the potatoes: Boil the potatoes until tender (about 15-20 minutes). Let them cool slightly then peel and chop into bite-sized pieces.

Prepare the vegetables: In a large bowl, combine the chopped potatoes, red onion, celery, hard-boiled eggs and pickles (if used).

Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper.

Combine and mix: Pour the dressing over the potato mixture and gently toss to coat everything evenly.

Chill and Garnish: Let the potato salad cool in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Avocado Salad
Ingredients

2 Cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
1 avocado, sliced
1 tablespoon sunflower seeds (optional for crunch)

Dressing

2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon lemon juice
Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures

Prepare the salad: Place the mixed greens in a large bowl.

Add toppings: Add the halved cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumbers, and avocado.

Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper.

Dress the Salad: Drizzle the dressing over the salad, and toss gently to combine.

Garnish: Sprinkle sunflower seeds on top for extra crunch.
